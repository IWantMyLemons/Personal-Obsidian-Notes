Definition:: finding limits by directly inserting the values

$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{x\to2}\frac{x^3-1}{x-1}&=\frac{2^3-1}{2-1}\\
&=\frac{7}{1}\\
&=7\\
\end{aligned}
$$
